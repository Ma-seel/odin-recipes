Lasagna Recipe

Description :

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with this classic lasagna recipe. Layers of tender, homemade pasta are nestled between rich, savory layers of seasoned ground beef and pork, simmered in a robust marinara sauce. Creamy, smooth ricotta cheese is blended with fresh basil and a hint of nutmeg, adding a delightful contrast to the meaty sauce. A generous layer of gooey mozzarella cheese, combined with a sprinkle of Parmesan, creates a golden, bubbly top thats irresistibly delicious. Each bite delivers a perfect balance of hearty meat, velvety cheese, and flavorful sauce, making this lasagna a true family favorite. Ideal for a cozy dinner or a special occasion, its sure to warm your heart and satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients :

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1/2 lb (225g) ground porkb (optional, can use all beef)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz/800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz/170g) tomato paste
  • 1 can (14 oz/400g) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) red wine(optional)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 15 oz (425g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 12 lasagna noodles(regular or no-boil, depending on preference)

Steps you should follow

Preparation :

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the Noodles:
    • For Regular Noodles: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and lay them flat on a sheet of parchment paper to prevent sticking.
    • For No-Boil Noodles:No need to cook. Simply use them directly in the layering process.
  3. Prepare the Cheese Mixture:
      4. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan cheese, parsley (if using), salt, and black pepper. Mix well and set aside.

    Make the Meat Sauce:

  4. Cook the Meat:
    • In a large skillet or saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the ground beef and ground pork (if using) and cook until browned, breaking up the meat with a spoon. Drain excess fat if necessary.
  5. Add Vegetables:
    • Add the chopped onion and garlic to the skillet. Cook until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant, about 2-3 minutes.

    Serve:

      This process will yield a hearty and flavorful lasagna perfect for any occasion!
